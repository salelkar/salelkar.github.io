About

Salelkar is a budding researcher in the making and her research interests lies at the intersaction of engineering and business for example- Communities of practice (COP), Knowledge Engineering (KE), Kowledge Flows, Knowledge Management Systems (KMS), Business Intelligence Systems (BIS).

Salelkar graduated from Goa University (Top 5 percentile) in 2014 with Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering . Salelkar was one of the 20 recipiet of Goa Scholars 2017 and graduated in 2018 Masters of Science from Chalmers University of Technology in Product Development .
