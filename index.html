Lakshmi Salelkar

About

Lakshmi Salelkar is a budding researcher in the making and her research interests lie at the intersection of engineering and business, for example: Complex Adaptive Systems, Communities of Practice (COP), Knowledge Management Systems (KMS), Business Models, Open data, Business Intelligence Systems (BIS). She graduated from Goa University (Top 5 percentile) in 2014 with Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering . She was one of the 20 recipients of Goa Scholars 2017 and graduated in 2018 with a Masters of Science in Product Development from Chalmers University of Technology.
