About

Lakshmi Salelkar is a Doctoral student at the Division of Industrial Engineering and Management, Ångströmslaboratoriet, Uppsala University. Her research focuses on the challenges that SME:s face while transitioning their production processes towards incorporating Additive Manufacting and Industry 4.0 technologies. She graduated from Goa University (Top 5 percentile) in 2014 with Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering . She was one of the 20 recipients of Goa Scholars 2017 and graduated in 2018 with a Masters of Science in Product Development from Chalmers University of Technology.
